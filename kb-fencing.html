FAQ

Good Questions, Straight Answers

Most residential fences in Round Rock require a permit from the city, especially if they exceed six feet in height or are located near an HOA. We can advise you on local requirements during your free estimate so there are no delays once work begins.
We install wood, wrought iron, vinyl/PVC, and chain link fencing. For Central Texas heat and humidity, wrought iron and vinyl tend to offer the longest lifespan with minimal maintenance, while wood provides the most natural look and is typically the most affordable upfront.
Most residential fence installations are completed in one to two days depending on the length, material, and site conditions. We give you a realistic timeline during the estimate and keep you updated throughout the project.
Yes — we are fully insured and bonded with up to $1 million in general liability coverage to protect your property during the job. Our 3-year workmanship warranty covers defects in installation, so if a post shifts or a panel separates due to our work, we'll fix it at no charge.
