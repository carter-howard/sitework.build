What We Offer

Our Services

Water Softener Installation
Hard water wreaking havoc on your pipes and appliances? We install high-quality water softening systems that protect your plumbing, extend the life of your fixtures, and give you cleaner, better-feeling water throughout your home.
Whole House Carbon Filters
Enjoy cleaner, healthier water from every tap in your home. Our whole house carbon filtration systems remove chlorine, sediment, and other contaminants for water you can feel good about using every day.
Reverse Osmosis (Point of Use)
Get hospital-grade drinking water right at your kitchen sink. Our reverse osmosis systems deliver ultra-purified water on demand, eliminating impurities that standard filters miss.
Water Heater Installation & Repair
Cold showers are never welcome. Whether you need a new water heater installed or your existing unit repaired, we get your hot water back fast — including tankless and traditional systems.
Leak Detection & Repair
Even a small leak can cause major damage over time. Our team uses proven techniques to locate hidden leaks quickly and repair them properly, saving you money and protecting your property.
Backflow Services
Protect your drinking water supply from contamination. We handle backflow prevention device installation, testing, and certification to keep your water safe and your property up to code.
Drain Cleaning
Slow or clogged drains don't stand a chance. We clear blockages thoroughly — not just temporarily — so your drains flow freely and stay that way.
Garbage Disposal Installation & Repair
A broken disposal can bring your kitchen to a halt. We install new units and repair existing ones quickly so you can get back to your routine without the mess or the hassle.
Tub & Shower Repair
Leaky faucets, faulty valves, or poor water pressure in your tub or shower? We diagnose and fix the problem right the first time, restoring full comfort and function to your bathroom.
Toilet Repair & Replacement
From running toilets to complete replacements, we handle it all efficiently and cleanly. We won't call it done until it's working perfectly — no matter the hour.
Bathroom Remodeling
Ready for a bathroom upgrade? We handle the plumbing side of your remodel from start to finish — rough-in, fixture installation, and final connections — so your vision comes to life without a hitch.
Residential & Commercial Plumbing
From single-family homes to commercial properties, Pickell Plumbing has the experience and manpower to handle plumbing projects of any size. One call covers it all.
Emergency Plumbing Services
Plumbing emergencies don't wait for business hours — and neither do we. We're available 24/7 and have been known to arrive in as little as 15 minutes when you need us most.
Water Line Repairs
A damaged water line can disrupt your entire home or business. We locate, diagnose, and repair water line issues quickly and correctly, minimizing disruption and preventing further damage.
Sewer Service
Sewer problems are serious. We provide thorough sewer line inspections, cleaning, and repairs to keep things flowing in the right direction and protect your property from costly backups.
