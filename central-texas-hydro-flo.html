Most septic systems benefit from a professional inspection and service at least once a year. Aerobic systems in Texas typically require quarterly maintenance per state regulations. Our annual maintenance contracts take the guesswork out by keeping you on a consistent, compliant schedule.
The aerator motor powers the air pump in an aerobic septic system, introducing oxygen to break down waste. Signs it may be failing include a continuously lit alarm, unusual noises from the system, or odors indicating poor treatment. We carry replacement motors on our trucks for fast same-day swaps.
